# Assess - Test Your Knowledge

Take skill assessments to evaluate your current knowledge and find the perfect learning path.

## Description
Interactive assessments that measure your expertise across 69 different roles. Get personalized recommendations based on your skills.

## Usage
```
/assess
```

## How Assessments Work

### 1. **Choose Assessment Type**
- **Role Assessment**: Evaluate fit for a specific role
- **Technology Assessment**: Test proficiency in a technology
- **Level Assessment**: Determine your overall level (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced)
- **Full Assessment**: Complete evaluation across all areas

### 2. **Take the Assessment**
- Multiple choice questions
- Practical coding challenges
- Real-world scenario problems
- Time-based tasks

### 3. **Get Results**
- Score and percentile ranking
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Recommended learning paths
- Personalized study plan

### 4. **Create Learning Plan**
- Based on assessment results
- Focused on your gaps
- Estimated time to competency
- Specific projects to build

## Assessment Examples

## Taking an Assessment

1. **Select Your Focus** - Choose role or technology
2. **Set Time Commitment** - Quick (20 min) or thorough (60 min)
3. **Start Assessment** - Answer questions honestly
4. **Review Results** - Understand your strengths/gaps
5. **Create Plan** - Get personalized learning path
6. **Start Learning** - Use `/learn` for curriculum

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Honest** - Accurate assessment requires honesty
2. **Don't Cheat** - You're learning for yourself
3. **Take Your Time** - No rushing, reflection is good
4. **Retake Later** - See how much you've improved
5. **Share Results** - Get community feedback
6. **Use Results** - Let them guide your learning

## Assessment Frequency

- **Baseline**: Take once when starting
- **Periodic**: Every 4-8 weeks to track progress
- **Before Learning**: Test knowledge before a course
- **After Learning**: Validate what you've learned
- **Career Change**: Test fit for new role
