## Recommended Browsing Sequences

### The Full-Stack Path
1. Start with a **Programming Language** (JavaScript/Python)
2. Learn **Frontend** fundamentals (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
3. Learn **Backend** with your chosen language
4. Progress to **Full-Stack** integrating both
5. Add **DevOps** for deployment and scaling

### The Data Science Path
1. Master **Python** and **Data Structures**
2. Learn **SQL** and database basics
3. Progress to **Data Analyst** or **Data Engineer**
4. Specialize in **Data Scientist** or **Machine Learning**
5. Lead teams as **AI Engineer** or **ML Ops**

### The System Architect Path
1. Deep expertise in **Backend** development
2. Master **System Design** and **API Design**
3. Learn **DevOps** and **Infrastructure**
4. Study **Software Architecture**
5. Lead as **Software Architect** or **CTO**
