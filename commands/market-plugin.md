# /market-plugin

Develop market strategy and growth tactics for your plugin with expert guidance from the Market Research Analyst and Growth Strategist.

## Usage

```
/market-plugin [plugin-name] [--research] [--positioning] [--strategy] [--launch]
```

## Examples

### Start market analysis
```
/market-plugin my-plugin
```

### Conduct market research
```
/market-plugin my-plugin --research
```

### Develop positioning
```
/market-plugin my-plugin --positioning
```

### Create growth strategy
```
/market-plugin my-plugin --strategy
```

### Plan launch
```
/market-plugin my-plugin --launch
```

## Market Strategy Process

### Phase 1: Market Research (--research)

Understand your market:
- Market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Competitive analysis
- User segmentation
- Trend analysis
- Opportunity identification
- Pricing benchmarking

Output:
- Market size report
- Competitive analysis
- Segment profiles
- Trend analysis
- Pricing recommendations

### Phase 2: Positioning (--positioning)

Define your position:
- Value proposition
- Target audience
- Competitive advantages
- Unique selling points
- Brand positioning
- Messaging framework

Output:
- Positioning statement
- Value proposition
- Messaging guide
- Elevator pitch
- Brand identity

### Phase 3: Growth Strategy (--strategy)

Plan your growth:
- Monetization model
- Pricing strategy
- Acquisition channels
- Retention tactics
- Partnership strategy
- Expansion roadmap

Output:
- Growth roadmap
- Acquisition strategy
- Pricing model
- Partnership plan
- Financial forecast

### Phase 4: Launch Planning (--launch)

Prepare for launch:
- Go-to-market plan
- Launch timeline
- Messaging strategy
- Launch channels
- PR strategy
- Success metrics

Output:
- Launch plan
- Marketing calendar
- PR strategy
- Metric dashboard
- Launch checklist

## Full Market Strategy

```
/market-plugin my-plugin --research --positioning --strategy --launch
```

Combines all phases for comprehensive market strategy.
